I don't remember my password.

Please press ‘Forgot Password?’ on the Sign In page. Biscuit will send you a temporary password via email. Please sign in with this password and change password immediately.

Please note that this temporary password will only be valid for 30 minutes.


A temporary password does not work.

In many cases, your email address is wrong. Biscuit send you a temporary even if there is no account of the email address. (for security)
Please try another email address to sign in.

Or you may have signed up with a email address with typo. Please send us email. We’ll find the correct email address you used.



Where is image to text?

We use the Evernote API to extract words from images. So we decide to provide the feature in a more proper way.

You must make Evernote notes including images instead of uploading a image in the Biscuit, and select ‘Import' from 'Settings/Data Transfer/Evernote’. Then Biscuit boxes with titles of Evernote notes and words of images will be created.
