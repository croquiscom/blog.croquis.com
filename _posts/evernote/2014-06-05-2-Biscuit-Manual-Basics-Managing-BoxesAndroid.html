This post will show you how to manage your boxes, which hold all your flashcards. 


To access your boxes tap on the icon button at the top left.  




Then the boxes menu should slide in. 




The Biscuit Box is the default box that first appears when you use Biscuit for the first time. 

Within the boxes menu you can add new boxes beyond the default Biscuit Box by pushing the plus sign right below your last box. You will be prompted to name your new box. 




You can rearrange the order of your boxes by holding your finger down on the far right side of a box and dragging up or down. 




To rename or delete a box hold your finger down on the middle of a box and the options should appear. Please note that deleting a box completely deletes all the flashcards within the box and does not send them to Memorized Words.
