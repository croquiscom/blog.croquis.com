Biscuit Manual: Advanced Feature - Bake It(iOS)

by Jason Lee

This post will cover Biscuit's Bake It feature for easily looking up definitions and creating flashcards for anything you are reading on your device. 


To turn on Bake it functionality please go into the Settings menu as shown in the pictures below. Use the switch to turn Bake it on or off.




When Bake it is on, you can switch to another app(such as Safari) and highlight then copy a word to see its definition pop up at the top of your screen, and it will be added as a flashcard to the box that is open within the app. 




You can also tap the definition notification to go straight to the Biscuit app and see the flashcard that was just created. 


Bake it Shut Off

Bake it will stop working if you close the Biscuit app. If you leave Biscuit running in the background for an extended period of time(about an hour) without using Bake it, the Bake it function will automatically shut off. If this happens, simply reopen the Biscuit app to have Bake it reactivated. 


Bake it Limitations

Bake it will not work in apps that block or limit the copy function. For example ebooks with DRM do not allow copy and paste therefore Biscuit will not be able to use Bake it when you view these ebooks.
