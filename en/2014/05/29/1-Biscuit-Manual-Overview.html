Biscuit Manual: Overview

by Jason Lee

What is Biscuit?

Biscuit is a multi-platform and cloud-based dictionary that focuses on enhancing the experience of finding and reviewing vocabulary words. Please check out our introduction video: 




You can download Biscuit for iOS and Android.  


Signing Up 

After downloading, using the links above, open up the app and you can then sign in using Facebook on your phone or you can join by providing an email and creating a password. 





Creating Flashcards 

To find words and create your first flashcards for studying, just swipe downwards in your Biscuit Box. 




Then just start typing to find words. Definitions appear automatically below the word. Press done or tap anywhere below the definition, and a flashcard will be saved in your box. 




Just Tap to Study 

To view the definition of a flashcard simply tap on any white area to the right of the word.




3 Simple Gesture Functions

After adding flashcards you can use simple gesture functions to keep track of your studying progress.

Slide a flashcard slightly to the right to mark it.  We suggest using this function to mark flashcards you are having trouble memorizing. 





Slide a flashcard slgihty to the left to grey it out (dim). We suggest using this function to mark flashcards you have become comfortable with, but don’t want to remove from your box yet. 




For flashcards you have mastered, completely slide a word to the left to send it to your memorized box and remove from your current box.
