Biscuit Manual: Basics - Flash Alerts

2014-06-09

This post will explain Biscuit’s flashcard alert functionality that is very useful for studying words throughout the day without having to constantly open the app. 


Flash Alerts are flashcard notifications that are sent from Biscuit even when the app is not running. The flashcards are pulled from your most recently used box(the one on screen when you close the app). The flashcards are selected at random for the notifications.  



To set Flash Alerts please use the settings menu to reach Flash Alerts as shown in the pictures below.



You will need to set the time period during which you would like to receive your Flash Alerts using the Start and Stop selectors. You can also set how many flashcards you want to receive(1 to 30) during this time period using the Flashcards selector. You can also choose to only receive marked flashcards within the box as Flash Alerts by using the Marked flashcards only button(see the Flashcard Gestures post to learn how to mark flashcards). 


Biscuit Manual: Basics - Managing Boxes(Android)

2014-06-05

This post will show you how to manage your boxes, which hold all your flashcards. 


To access your boxes tap on the icon button at the top left.  




Then the boxes menu should slide in. 




The Biscuit Box is the default box that first appears when you use Biscuit for the first time. 

Within the boxes menu you can add new boxes beyond the default Biscuit Box by pushing the plus sign right below your last box. You will be prompted to name your new box. 




You can rearrange the order of your boxes by holding your finger down on the far right side of a box and dragging up or down. 




To rename or delete a box hold your finger down on the middle of a box and the options should appear. Please note that deleting a box completely deletes all the flashcards within the box and does not send them to Memorized Words. 
 

Biscuit Manual: Basics - Managing Boxes(iOS)

2014-06-05

This post will show you how to manage your boxes, which hold all your flashcards. 


To access your boxes tap on the menu button at the top left.  




Then the boxes menu should slide in. 




The Biscuit Box is the default box that first appears when you use Biscuit for the first time. 

Within the boxes menu you can add new boxes beyond the default Biscuit Box by pushing the plus sign right below your last box. You will be prompted to name your new box. 




You will need to enter edit mode to use the functions mentioned below. Please tap the edit button at the top right of the screen to enter edit mode, which should look like this:




You can rearrange the order of your boxes by holding your finger down on the middle of a box and dragging up or down. 




To rename a box, tap on the current name of the box to modify. 




To delete a box, tap on the red circles that appear next to the boxes in Edit Mode then tap the red delete button that appears. Please note that deleting a box completely deletes all the flashcards within the box and does not send them to Memorized Words.
