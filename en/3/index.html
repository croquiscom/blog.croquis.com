Biscuit Manual: Settings - Voice and Sound

2014-06-11

This post will cover Biscuit’s Voice and Sound settings. 


Using Voice and Sound settings you can activate voice pronunciations and Biscuit’s sound effects. To reach the Voice and Sound settings please use the settings menu as  shown in the pictures below. 


Voice Data

Downloading voice data will produce an audible english pronunciation of the selected words in your box whenever you flip the relevant flashcard. To download voice data simply push the No Voice Data button. The process is two steps please wait for step 1 (1/2) to reach 100% before moving to another screen or app. Please note this download will add close to 400MB to the Biscuit app. After downloading, the button will be transformed into a Voice switch that will allow you turn voice pronunciations on and off. 




Sound Effects

Biscuit has sound effects that accompany certain actions/gestures such as sliding a flashcard to memorized or pulling down to add a flashcard. You can turn these sound effects on or off by using the Sound Effects switch.
